Bush Turkey Classic

A three round, balls-to-the-wall, short course running series.

Welcome to the inaugural Bush Turkey Classic. A 3 round, short course running series on some iconic tracks in the Moreton Bay region. Points will be given for the 3 Rounds in the cross country format where you get a point corresponding to your finishing position. As permission from the council or any other organisation will be not be sought, numbers are limited to those willing to have a red hot crack!

  • ROUND ONE

Round One: Boondall Wetlands

SATURDAY, OCTOBER 30, 2021 AT 6 AM

Round 1 will be held at the Boondall Wetlands for a 1 lap, balls to the wall, clockwise Loop. Rock up at 5:30 for a warm up and stretch. Men's race will commence at 6am and women's at 6:15 so babysitters not required.
